Good healthy food

The first recipe is not actually food, it is for a nice cup of tea:

  1. Warm the cup with boiling water
  2. Brew the tea bag in fresh water for 3 minutes
  3. Remove the tea bag. Add sugar if you don't actually want to taste the tea (grr...)
  4. Enjoy a nice cup of tea and all is better :)

The ingredients are pretty straight forward:

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk
  • And that's all...
