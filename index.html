Tea

The first recipe is not actually food, it is for a nice cup of tea:

  1. Warm the cup with boiling water
  2. Brew the tea bag in fresh water for 3 minutes
  3. Remove the tea bag. Add sugar if you don't actually want to taste the tea (grr...)
  4. Enjoy a nice cup of tea and all is better :)

The ingredients are pretty straight forward:

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk
  • And that's all...

For an alternative, and far more detailed recipe for making tea, I recommend George Orwells essay A Nice Cup of Tea

Scrambled Eggs

The second recipe is actually the first actual food recipe, it is for scrambled eggs:

  1. Warm olive oil in a frying pan while beating the egg, milk and pepper together
  2. Pour the milky, peppery eggs into the frying pan
  3. Stir occasionally, until cooked
  4. Serve on some buttered toast, and some tea, for a great start to the day :)

The ingredients are pretty straight forward again...:

  • Eggs
  • Milk (which you already have for the tea!)
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
Breakfast Biscuits and Coffee

The third recipe is for food again, but for days when sleep is good and work starts soon :(

  1. Before you leave the house, pick up the breakfast biscuits and the coffee money
  2. Spend the coffee money on a coffee (complicated this!) Tea is a rip-off in coffee shops and they don't even do it right!
  3. Read e-mails at your desk, eating the breakfast biscuits and sipping coffee

The ingredients are really simple:

  • Breakfast biscuits
  • Money for a coffee (£1.70 for me)
  • The dream of the weekend
